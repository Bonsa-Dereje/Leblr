What is Leblr ?

Leblr is an all-in-one, AI-powered media workflow platform designed for churches, ministries, and sermon-driven content creators. Whether you’re capturing footage on a phone, syncing audio in the booth, editing in Premiere Pro, or posting to social media — Leblr streamlines the entire process, helping you focus more on the message and less on the mechanics.

Mark Timeline

Mark powerful sermon moments as they happen. Flags emotional or high-engagement segments so you can turn them into reels instantly.

Multitrack Recording

Record multiple audio tracks using different phones as sound cards. Each phone syncs automatically as a node, with live meters and noise reduction.

One-Tap Social Export

Publish to YouTube, Instagram, or your website in seconds. Leblr formats your edits perfectly for every platform — no resizing or re-exporting needed.
